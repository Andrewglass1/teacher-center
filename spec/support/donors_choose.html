My Students: Do you get sentimental when you think back to the classic stories you remember from elementary school? Do you have a book that changed your life? A book that you insist your own children read? My students need those books! They need the classics!

My students are bright, motivated, and ready to learn, but they need your help. They attend an open-enrollment school with 95% free or reduced lunch. Students at my school come from low-income homes whose families have not been able to receive the education they deserved. Our school's mission is to get our students to college, whatever it takes, no matter what the challenges may be.

My Project: With the resource of these classic and well-written children's stories and poetry, my students will have access to stories that will aid in their comprehension, development, experience and breadth of knowledge, and above all, serve as resources and exemplars for them to model their own writing after. The best writers are the readers who read the good stuff.

This project is so important. There's a dire and desperate need for kids to have books in their hands NOW! Kids need to read. In elementary school, we are learning to read, but as adolescents and adults, we read to learn. hide»

My students need children's books to support their comprehension and to model their own work after these classics. details»
